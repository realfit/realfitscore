Why these tests

Drawn from the best
standards in the world.

The 13 tests in the Absolute Score weren't invented — they were selected. Each one is a proven, well-established measure used across military fitness programs, professional sports combines, first responder screening, and competitive athletics. Together they cover the full picture.

No single test tells the whole story. A great bench press doesn't tell you anything about endurance. A fast 40 doesn't tell you about strength. The Absolute Score combines tests across six distinct components — cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, muscular endurance, power, speed, and agility — because genuine fitness requires all of them.

The score is deliberately absolute. No adjustments for age, gender, or background. A 700 earned at 25 means exactly the same thing as a 700 earned at 45 — because the only benchmark that tells you whether your training is actually working is one that doesn't move unless you do.

Military & first responders
Push-ups, pull-ups, shuttle run, 400m — proven standards used to screen fitness for service.
Pro sports combines
40 yard dash, bench press, standing long jump, shuttle run — the same tests used to evaluate elite athletes at the highest level.
General fitness
5K run, squat, plank, wall sit, med ball throw — accessible tests that measure real-world fitness for anyone.

Estimated vs Certified

Two ways to get your score.

The free estimator uses the same scoring engine as the certified app — same tests, same math. The difference is who administered the tests.

Certified result

Administered. Verified. Credentialed.

A REALFIT Certified Official administers every test in person, according to FITDAT protocol. The certified result is the only class of REALFIT result recognised for institutional, competitive, or professional use.

Think of the difference between self-reported income and verified income on a loan application. Same data type. Fundamentally different institutional trust.

Open methodology

We exposed every decision.

Golf has a handicap. Finance has a credit score. Fitness has never had an equivalent — until now. The REALFIT Absolute Score is our proposal: a single, comparable number for overall physical fitness, built on an absolute standard with no adjustments for age, gender, or background.

Every test selection, weighting decision, scoring table, and normalization method is documented in full and publicly available on FITDAT. No black box.

If you find a flaw, we want to know. If you want to build a different score set on the same tests — different weights, different purpose — the tools are there.
